      SSL Certificate Types at a Glance

      Domain Validation (DV)

      Fastest issuance in minutes. Verifies domain ownership only. Best for blogs and personal sites. No business verification required.

      Organization Validation (OV)

      Verifies domain and business info. Takes 1-3 days. Ideal for business websites needing added credibility and visitor trust.

      Extended Validation (EV)

      Full legal business verification. Takes 1-7 days. Best for e-commerce, banking, and sites handling sensitive customer data.
