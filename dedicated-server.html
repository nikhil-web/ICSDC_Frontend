      Your Digital Servers: Secured by ICSDC

      Your dedicated server requires protection at every layer. We don’t just provide tools; we deliver managed security services built and monitored by our experts to keep your data safe 24/7.

      Firewall as a Service (FWaaS)

      Intelligent security gate filtering traffic at the network level, blocking threats before they reach your hardware.

      Secures your administrative layers and locks the master keys. MFA prevents access even if a password is stolen.

      Web Application Firewall (WAF)

      Ensures all data transferred between the server and users is strongly encrypted, building customer trust and compliance.
