      The Advantages of Linux Cloud Hosting

      Linux is the foundation of the modern internet. Here is why the world's most demanding infrastructure runs on Linux — and why yours should too.

      Open Source Freedom

      No licensing fees, no vendor lock-in. Linux gives you complete freedom to customize your environment and run any open-source software stack.

      Linux's lean kernel and minimal overhead translate to faster response times, higher concurrency, and more efficient resource utilization across all workloads.

      Superior Security

      A mature permission model, rapid security patching, SELinux, AppArmor, and a transparent open-source codebase make Linux the most secure server OS.

      Cost Effective

      Massive Community

      Millions of developers, billions of packages, and decades of documentation. If you hit a challenge on Linux, the answer already exists somewhere.

      Enterprise Proven

      From Google and Amazon to NASA and the NYSE — Linux powers the world's most demanding infrastructure. It is the enterprise-grade choice.
