      THE PROCESS

      How ICSDC PAM / MFA Works

      1

      Identify Privileged Users and Systems

      ICSDC PAM discovers and maps all privileged accounts across servers, applications, cloud platforms, and DevOps tools.

      2

      Secure and Store Credentials

      Privileged credentials are securely vaulted, encrypted, and removed from hard-coded files or shared access points.
